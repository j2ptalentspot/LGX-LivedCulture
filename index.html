Our Story & Mission

Built by practitioners,
for practitioners

Practitioners in leadership development, cultural transformation, OD interventions, behavioral science, and workplace behavior change are shaping the future here. Step in — join the network →

Most organisations invest significantly in defining their culture — values on walls, leadership frameworks, competency models. Yet the gap between what is said and what is lived remains one of the most persistent challenges in organisational life.

We foresee a huge transformation in the way people learn, grow, and develop — and hence, organisations will unexpectedly change the ways of implementing their learning and OD interventions in line with the pace of technological change.

LivedCulture exists to close that gap — not through more training, but through behavioural science applied at the moment that matters. LivedCulture is precisely addressing the challenge head-on to co-create the future through hyper-focused learning strategies, micro-interventions, micro-coaching and highly customised programs.

By joining this network, you are not just a user — you are a co-creator of the world's most practitioner-validated behavioural change movement.

About LivedCulture

The open sandbox

Where top L&D, OD, and Coaching minds co-create the future

Most organisations invest significantly in defining their culture — values on walls, leadership frameworks, competency models. Yet the gap between what is said and what is lived remains one of the most persistent challenges in organisational life.

With the democratisation of learning, the way learning, OD & coaching is being curated and delivered today will see a dramatic change in time to come. We foresee a huge transformation in the way people learn, grow, develop — and hence, organisations will unexpectedly change the ways of implementing their learning and OD interventions in line with the pace of technological changes.

We believe human growth shouldn't be locked away for a select few. This group aims to envisage, envision and curate today's coaches, facilitators, L&D & OD practitioners to be future-ready.

This is a curated community of select coaches, facilitators, OD/L&D leaders, and HR professionals focused on building sustainable value with real impact on leadership, culture, and organisational growth.

Terms of Service & Content License

Last updated: June 2025

1. Membership

By joining the LivedCulture Practitioner Network, you confirm that the information you provide is accurate and that you are a practicing professional in coaching, facilitation, L&D, OD, HR, or a related behavioural science discipline.

2. Content License

Any frameworks, nudge blueprints, interventions, case studies, or content you contribute are licensed to LivedCulture on a perpetual, non-exclusive basis. Your authorship and attribution will always be preserved. You retain ownership of your original intellectual property.

3. Use of Your Profile

With your consent, your professional profile will be listed in the LivedCulture Practitioner Directory. You may request removal at any time by writing to team@lgx-livedculture.org.

4. Commercial Opportunities

LivedCulture may connect you with organisations seeking your expertise. Any commercial engagement will be governed by a separate agreement. LivedCulture does not take a commission unless separately agreed in writing.

5. Privacy

Your personal data will never be sold to third parties. It will be used solely for network communications, opportunity matching, and community updates.

6. Code of Conduct

Members are expected to engage respectfully, share knowledge in good faith, and uphold the highest professional standards.

7. Contact

For any questions, write to team@lgx-livedculture.org
