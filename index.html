1. Membership

By joining the LivedCulture Practitioner Network, you confirm that the information you provide is accurate and that you are a practicing professional in the fields of coaching, facilitation, L&D, OD, HR, or a related behavioral science discipline.

2. Content License

Any frameworks, nudge blueprints, interventions, case studies, or content you contribute to the LivedCulture platform are licensed to LivedCulture on a perpetual, non-exclusive basis. Your authorship and attribution will always be preserved. You retain ownership of your original intellectual property.

3. Use of Your Profile

With your consent, your professional profile (name, role, city, expertise areas) will be listed in the LivedCulture Practitioner Directory. You may request removal at any time by writing to team@lgx-livedculture.org.

4. Commercial Opportunities

LivedCulture may connect you with organisations seeking your expertise. Any commercial engagement that results from such a connection will be governed by a separate agreement between you and the client. LivedCulture does not take a commission unless separately agreed in writing.

5. Privacy

Your personal data (email, mobile) will never be sold to third parties. It will be used solely for platform communications, opportunity matching, and community updates. You may unsubscribe or request data deletion at any time.

6. Code of Conduct

Members are expected to engage respectfully, share knowledge in good faith, and uphold the highest professional standards. LivedCulture reserves the right to remove any member who violates these principles.

7. Contact

For any questions regarding these terms, write to team@lgx-livedculture.org

About LivedCulture

Let's bridge the gap between
perceived culture & lived culture

Most organisations invest significantly in defining their culture — values on walls, leadership frameworks, competency models. Yet the gap between what is said and what is lived remains one of the most persistent challenges in organisational life.

LivedCulture exists to close that gap — not through more training, but through behavioural science applied at the moment that matters.

What we're building
SHIFT A micro-intervention and nudge engine that drives real behavioural change — in under 60 seconds a day.
MIRROR A diagnostic platform that shows organisations the gap between their stated and lived culture.
COACH An AI-assisted coaching layer that personalises development for leaders at every level.
LEAD A progressive leadership journey built on spaced learning, peer accountability, and real-world application.
Why a practitioner network?

The platform is being built with practitioners — not for them. We believe the most credible, battle-tested behavioural interventions come from coaches, facilitators, and L&D leaders who have seen what works in the real world. By joining this network, you are not just a user — you are a co-creator of the world's most practitioner-validated behavioural change platform.

The science foundation
· Self-Determination Theory (Deci & Ryan)
· Nudge Theory (Thaler & Sunstein)
· Habit formation (BJ Fogg)
· Spaced repetition (Ebbinghaus)
· Psychological safety (Edmondson)
· Neuroplasticity (Lally, UCL)
"Culture is not what you say it is. It is what people do when no one is watching — and what they do when everyone is."

If you are a practitioner who believes that sustainable change is behavioural, not just intellectual — this network is built for you.
